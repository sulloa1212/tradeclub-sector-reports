6How to Read This Report

Quick reference guide for all the scores and labels used above.

Direction Score (−100 to +100) How bullish or bearish a sector looks right now. +100 = strongest possible bullish signal across all 5 inputs. −100 = maximum bearish. 0 = neutral / no edge. This is a relative ranking tool — compare sectors to each other, not to an absolute standard.
Conviction: High / Medium / Low How confident the analysis is in the direction score. High = multiple inputs align clearly. Medium = some mixed signals. Low = weak or insufficient data. Low conviction means "neutral / no clean setup" — don't force a trade.
Swing-Conviction Score (0–100) How likely a stock is to move HARD in the sector's direction over the 3-day to 6-week swing window. Technically-led: chart, momentum, volume, and relative strength drive this score. 0 = no swing edge; 100 = maximum swing conviction.
Fundamental Health Score (0–100) A separate measure of the company's underlying business health — revenue growth, EPS growth, margins, FCF, balance sheet, analyst revisions, and guidance. It is independent of the chart. ≥65 = green / healthy. 45–64 = amber / neutral. <45 = red / weak.
⛽ Fuel Tag (Longs) Health ≥65 → ⛽ ADDS FUEL (business confirms the chart). 45–64 → NEUTRAL (no extra boost or drag). <45 → ⚠ FIGHTS TREND (a weak company — the fundamentals work against a long position).
⛽ Fuel Tag (Short-or-Avoid) Health ≤40 → ⛽ ADDS FUEL (weak fundamentals add to the short thesis). 41–60 → NEUTRAL. >60 → ⚠ FIGHTS TREND (shorting a healthy company is riskier — say so and size down).
⚠ Earnings In Window If a stock's next earnings date falls within the swing window (roughly the next 6 weeks), it is flagged with an amber ⚠ IN WINDOW badge. This means there is event risk — the stock could gap large in either direction on the earnings date. Options strategies or reduced position sizing may be appropriate. Always confirm the earnings date with your own brokerage.
Color Code ■ Green = bullish / long direction.
■ Red = bearish / short-or-avoid direction.
