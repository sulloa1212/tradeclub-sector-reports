Direction Score (−100 to +100)

Conviction (High / Medium / Low)
How much the inputs agree. High = multiple inputs pointing the same direction, clean chart, clear catalyst. Low = conflicting signals, choppy tape, or thin data. Low conviction means "trend is X, but the setup is messy" — size accordingly.
Swing-Conviction Score (0–100)
For individual stocks — how likely this stock is to move the hardest in its sector's direction over 3 days to 6 weeks. It's technically led: trend alignment (35%), relative strength (25%), catalyst & earnings proximity (20%), move-size potential / beta (20%). Higher = more likely to make the biggest move. It is NOT a quality rating.
Fundamental Health Score (0–100)
Absolute business quality, separate from the chart. Combines revenue growth (20%), EPS growth & beat history (20%), margins (15%), free cash flow & balance sheet (15%), analyst revisions (10%), guidance/backlog (10%), dividend/capital returns (10%). Think of it as "how healthy is this company's business right now?"
