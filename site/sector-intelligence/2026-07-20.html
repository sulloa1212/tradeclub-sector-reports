7How to Read This Report

A quick legend so every number on every card makes sense.

Direction Score (−100 to +100)
The analyst's aggregate read on a sector's direction. Combines five weighted inputs: price trend (30%), relative strength vs SPY (25%), macro tailwind/headwind (20%), news/catalyst flow (15%), and momentum/breadth (10%). Higher = more bullish. This is the analyst's judgment, not an algorithmic output.
Conviction: High / Medium / Low
How clean the evidence is. High = multiple confirming signals, clear price structure, verified catalysts. Low = one or two factors, mixed signals, or thin data. Low conviction is a real warning — treat those setups with extra skepticism.
Swing-Conviction Score (0–100)
How likely this specific stock is to move HARDEST in the sector's direction over the 3-day–6-week window. Weights: technical setup quality (35%), relative strength (25%), catalyst/earnings proximity (20%), move-potential/beta (20%). Higher = more likely to lead the sector's move.
Fundamental Health Score (0–100)
Separate from the swing score — this measures the absolute health of the underlying business: revenue growth (20%), EPS growth & beat history (20%), margins (15%), FCF & balance sheet (15%), analyst revisions (10%), guidance/backlog (10%), capital returns (10%). It does NOT predict near-term price action — it tells you whether the business supports the direction.
⛽ Fuel Tags
Longs: Health ≥65 → ⛽ ADDS FUEL (business supports the bull case) · 45–64 → NEUTRAL · <45 → ⚠ FIGHTS TREND (great setup but risky business).
Shorts/Avoid: Health ≤40 → ⛽ ADDS FUEL (weak business supports the bear case) · 41–60 → NEUTRAL · >60 → ⚠ FIGHTS TREND (healthy company is a riskier short).
Earnings-in-Window Flag
Any earnings date falling within roughly the next 6 weeks is flagged ⚠ DATE in amber. This means there is binary event risk — the stock could gap dramatically up or down on the print regardless of the setup. Reduce size or paper-trade first. "✓ Reported" means Q2 is already out — lower event risk this window.
Green / Amber / Red Meters
Swing-Conviction meter: green ≥65, amber 45–64, red <45.
Fundamental Health meter: green ≥65, amber 45–64, red <45.
Higher is not automatically "good" — in a bearish sector, a high Fundamental Health score (red company = healthy business = ⚠ FIGHTS TREND for a short) is a warning, not a green light.
