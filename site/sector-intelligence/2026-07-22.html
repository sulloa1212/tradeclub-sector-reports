7How to Read This Report

Direction Score (−100 to +100): A composite reading of each sector's bullishness or bearishness. Think of it like a temperature gauge. +100 = maximum bullish; −100 = maximum bearish; 0 = perfectly neutral. Built from five weighted inputs: trend/price structure (30%), relative strength vs SPY (25%), macro tailwind/headwind (20%), news & catalyst flow (15%), and momentum/breadth (10%).
