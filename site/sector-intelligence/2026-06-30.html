How to Read This Report

A quick reference for the methodology behind every number. This is the "how we reason" section — the actual results (scores, readings, stock picks) are in the sections above.

Direction Score (−100 → +100)

A composite score for each sector's swing-trading direction. Positive = bullish bias, negative = bearish bias. Computed from 5 weighted inputs: Trend & price structure (30%), Relative strength vs SPY (25%), Macro tailwind/headwind (20%), News & catalyst flow (15%), Momentum/breadth (10%). Scores are not predictions — they reflect the weight of current evidence.

Conviction Level

High: Multiple inputs aligned, clean trend, verified catalyst — high confidence in the direction. Medium: More inputs agree than disagree, but a meaningful counter-case exists. Low: Mixed signals; direction is a lean, not a conviction — reduce size or avoid. When conviction is Low and score is near zero, we say "NEUTRAL / no clean setup."

Swing-Conviction Score (Stock, 0–100)

How strongly we expect this specific stock to move in the sector's direction over the 3-day to 6-week swing horizon. Technically led (chart > story): Technical setup quality 35%, Relative strength 25%, Catalyst & news 20%, Move-strength potential (beta/liquidity) 20%. Higher = stronger expected move in the sector direction, bigger or smaller — not better or worse.

Fundamental Health Score (Stock, 0–100)

How healthy the underlying business is, independently of the chart. Revenue growth 20%, EPS growth & beat history 20%, Margins 15%, FCF & balance sheet 15%, Analyst revisions 10%, Guidance/backlog 10%, Dividend/capital returns 10% (non-payers: reallocated to growth/FCF). This is absolute business quality — green ≥65 / amber 45–64 / red <45. It does NOT replace the swing score.

⛽ Fuel Tag

Does the business quality support or fight the swing direction? For Longs: Health ≥65 → ⛽ ADDS FUEL (business confirms chart); 45–64 → NEUTRAL; <45 → ⚠ FIGHTS TREND (weak business = riskier long). For Shorts-or-Avoid: Health ≤40 → ⛽ ADDS FUEL (weak business confirms short); 41–60 → NEUTRAL; >60 → ⚠ FIGHTS TREND (shorting a strong business — extra risk).

⚠ Earnings-in-Window Flag

If a stock reports earnings within the 3 day–6 week swing window, it gets an amber warning badge. Earnings create binary, unpredictable price moves that can overpower any technical setup in either direction. You can choose to: (a) avoid the position entirely, (b) exit before the date, or (c) size very small knowing the outcome is a coin flip. Never ignore this flag.
