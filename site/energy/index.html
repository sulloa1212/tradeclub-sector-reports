How aligned the 5 scoring inputs are. High = all arrows point the same way. Medium = mixed signals, one clear dominant theme. Low = genuinely choppy / no clean setup.

This is the technical driver for stock selection. How strongly is this stock set up to move in the sector's direction? 70+ = high confidence setup. Below 50 = weaker setup. This score picks the stocks.

💼 Fundamental Health Score (0–100)

Separate from swing score. This answers "is the underlying company strong?" A score of 65+ means a healthy business. For LONGS it adds fuel to the technical trade. For SHORTS it's a warning — shorting a strong company is riskier.

For LONG picks: "Adds Fuel" means the strong business reinforces the bullish technical setup. For SHORT/AVOID picks: "Fights Trend" means the company is fundamentally healthy — the bear case must rest on price/cycle reasons, not company weakness.

Short-or-Avoid

In a bearish sector, the top picks are names likely to move most to the downside. "Short" requires a brokerage margin account; "Avoid" means don't initiate or hold a long position. Both are valid responses.
